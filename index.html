About Me

I am a first year PhD student in the Division of Computational & Data Sciences at Washington University in St. Louis. I am interested in applying machine learning and data-driven approaches to study neurological and neuropsychiatric disorders. Currently, I am rotating in the lab of Dr. Aristeidis Sotiras.

I have a background in wet lab & clinical neuroscience research. I studied biology and neuroscience at Grinnell College, as well as psychiatric research at King's College London. I have also held research assistant positions at Washington University and the National Institutes of Health.

Outside of lab, I enjoy making & listening to music, working on Python projects, playing chess, and spending time with my cat and dog.
